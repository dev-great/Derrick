The Renaissance Home, is a non-profit organisation, whose dream and aim is to diminish the scaring effects of poverty in the lives of children and orphans. Tocreate a good standard of living and a strong, social, educational, physical and mental structure for children.
Let’s Start

Generosity consists not of the sum given, but the manner in
which it is bestowed.

  1. 1. The Renaissance home believes that home is not a location but a place, where true happiness, comfort, hope, is found and easily accessed, characterized by an effort to always put the well being of a child first.
  2. 2. The window of accomplished dreams and aspiration for the less privileged children.
  3. 3. To work and affiliated with other National and International Charitable Organisations to nurture and support children in overcoming the obstacles caused by the chains of proverty, domestic and social violence, diseases and discrimination.
  4. 4. To provide a UN safe standard of living for Children in Nigeria.
  5. 5. To groom children who would be become law abiding citizens and the bright future of our country Nigeria.
  6. 6. To provide job opportunities for multiple individuals in different fields and careers.
